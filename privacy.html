Privacy Policy for StickerOptimizer

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Marius Girutis develops and offers the StickerOptimizer mobile application (the "App"). This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information from users of the App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

StickerOptimizer is a simple calculator app and does not collect, use, or store any personal data from you or your device. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information. The app does not require access to your camera, photos, or contacts.

2. Data Processing

The App performs all calculations and data processing locally on your device. None of the data you input into the calculator is ever transmitted to our servers or to any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks within the App that would collect or share your personal information. Your data remains completely on your device.

4. Consent

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: ccholio@icloud.com.
